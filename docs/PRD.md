# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Harbour.Space Platform Stack

## 1. Project Overview & Objectives
The goal is to build a high-fidelity, functional clone of the Harbour.Space application ecosystem. This includes the public-facing marketing layer, the application/lead funnel, the authenticated student portal reflecting the unique 3-week modular learning schedule, and background operational workflows (Mock CRM & Email).

---

## 2. Epics & Functional Requirements

### Epic 1: Marketing & Program Pages (Logged-Out)
*   **Homepage:** Premium landing experience capturing the "Tech, Entrepreneurship, and Design" brand ethos. Features global campus indicators (Barcelona & Bangkok) and key statistics (e.g., ICPC Gold Medallists, 95% Employability).
*   **Program Catalog & Detail Pages:** Dynamic routing for degrees across Technology (Computer Science, Data Science, Front-End) and Creative tracks (Interaction Design, High-Tech Entrepreneurship).
    *   *Requirement:* Display the "14 modules, 3 weeks per module" learning structure visually.
*   **Call-to-Action (CTA) & Lead Capture:** Interactive "Apply Now" and "Download Brochure" modals/flows capturing user contact information.

### Epic 2: Authentication & Multi-Role Experience
*   **Authentication Flow:** JWT or session-based Login/Signup experience. 
*   **Logged-In Dashboard (Student Hub):**
    *   **Current Module View:** Displays the active 3-week course, remaining days, assigned industry mentor, and daily schedule (3 hours of interactive sessions/day).
    *   **Academic Timeline:** Progress track across the 14 yearly modules (Completed, Active, Upcoming).
    *   **Profile/Documents:** Status of English proficiency certificate, transcripts, and visa handling checklist.

### Epic 3: Mock CRM & Admin Management
*   **Admin Dashboard:** A simple interface to view leads generated by the marketing CTA flows.
*   **Application Pipeline:** Track applications moving through stages: `Submitted` ➔ `Interview Scheduled` ➔ `Accepted` ➔ `Enrolled`.
*   **Data Persistence:** Store lead profiles (Name, Email, Program Interest, Campus Choice) in a unified database schema.

### Epic 4: Analytics, Automation & Mock Workflows
*   **Mock Email Service:** An internal portal/logger showing outbound automated emails triggered by user actions:
    *   *Trigger A:* "Application Received" instant confirmation email upon submitting an application.
    *   *Trigger B:* "Interview Invitation" from the admissions team once an admin advances their status.
*   **Analytics Dashboard:** Frontend event logging tracking clicks on "Apply Now", time spent on specific program pages, and application conversion funnel dropout rates.
